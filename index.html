Seong Han

I am a research assistant at NYU Laboratory for Vision and Action, working on computational models of the visual system with Prof. Michael Landy. My research interests intersect mathematical modeling and machine learning, with an emphasis on healthcare and biology.

Previously, I was at NYU where I received a B.A. degree in Mathematics. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Charlie Peskin and Prof. Charles Puelz on mathematical modeling and simulation in congenital heart disease at the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences.
